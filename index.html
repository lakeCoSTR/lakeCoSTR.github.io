Welcome to lakeCoSTR, a user-friendly tool to access the Landsat (LS) Collection 2 temperature product for LS missions 4-8.

lakeCoSTR was created by Christina Herrick (University of New Hampshire, christina.herrick@unh.edu) and Bethel Steele (Cary Institute, steeleb@caryinstitute.org).

Read the lakeCoSTR manuscript in Ecosphere [here]]https://esajournals.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/full/10.1002/ecs2.4357).
